### Buyer's Actions

#### Inquiry (Anfrage)

> **DEFINITION:** An **Inquiry** is a request for information. It is legally NON-binding.

**General Inquiry (Allgemeine Anfrage):** Customer wants overview (catalogs, price lists).

**Specific Inquiry (Spezielle Anfrage):** Customer has concrete wishes and wants a specific offer.

#### Order (Bestellung)

The buyer's request to the seller to supply goods. Can be in response to an offer or on buyer's initiative.
