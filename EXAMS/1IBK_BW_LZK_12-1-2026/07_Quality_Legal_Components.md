## Fungible vs Non-Fungible Goods

ğŸ’¡ **KEY POINT:** The type of goods determines which quality definition method to use!

### Fungible Products (Vertretbare Produkte)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Interchangeable; quality evident from one piece |
| Quality Methods | Samples, brands, types, standards |
| Examples | Mobile phones, new cars, clothing, DIN A4 paper |

### Non-Fungible Products (Nicht vertretbare Produkte)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Individually made; each piece unique |
| Quality Methods | Inspection, description, illustration |
| Examples | Art, real estate, used cars, custom furniture |

## Methods of Quality Definition

### 1. Inspection (Besichtigung)

- Buyer examines product directly
- Important for unique items, used goods, products with defects
- Contract phrase: "Wie besichtigt" (as inspected)

### 2. Description & Illustration (Beschreibung und Abbildung)

- Text and images explain characteristics
- Common in online shops

### 3. Samples & Specimens (Muster und Proben)

> **DEFINITION:** **Muster** = Small quantities showing quality of the whole
> **DEFINITION:** **Proben** = Free samples for consumption/testing

| Type | German | Description | Return Right |
|------|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Purchase by Sample | Kauf nach Probe/Muster | Product must match sample | If doesn't match |
| Purchase on Approval | Kauf auf Probe | Return within set period | Yes |
| Purchase for Testing | Kauf zur Probe | Small quantity to test | No |

### 4. Brands & Types (Marken und Typen)

> **DEFINITION:** **Marken** = Protected signs distinguishing products from competitors

**Types of Marks:**
- Word marks (Coca-Cola)
- Pictorial marks (Apple logo)
- Combined marks (BMW logo)

ğŸ’¡ **KEY POINT:** The Trademark Protection Act (Markenschutzgesetz) protects brands.

> **DEFINITION:** **Typen** = Similar products from one company (iPhone 16, iPhone 16 Pro)

## Special Purchase Types

### Spezifikationskauf (Purchase by Specification)

> **DEFINITION:** Quality roughly defined; buyer specifies details (quantity, dimensions) within a deadline.

**Example:** Fashion store orders 1000 jeans, sizes specified, but color to be determined by March 1st.

### Kauf in Bausch und Bogen (Purchase in Bulk)

> **DEFINITION:** Buyer takes entire goods at one price regardless of defects. Seller not liable for specific quality.

ğŸ’¡ **KEY POINT:** Goods must not be unusable or only consist of worst quality.

**Example:** Sale of entire grape harvest in bulk.
