## Contract Types Overview

üí° **KEY POINT:** Each contract type has a specific **Leistung (performance)** and **Gegenleistung (consideration)**.

| Contract Type | German | Performance | Consideration |
|---------------|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Sales Contract | Kaufvertrag | Product | Purchase Price |
| Work Contract | Werkvertrag | Work/Service | Fee (Werklohn) |
| Rental Contract | Mietvertrag | Leased object | Rent (Mietzins) |
| Employment Contract | Arbeitsvertrag | Work performance | Wage/Salary |
| Loan Agreement | Kreditvertrag | Money amount | Interest + Repayment |
| Insurance Contract | Versicherungsvertrag | Insurance benefit | Premium |
