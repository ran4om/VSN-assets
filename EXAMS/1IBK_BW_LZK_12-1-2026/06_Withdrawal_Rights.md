## Special Withdrawal Rights

‚ö° **EXAM TIP:** These apply to specific situations only!

### When Do Special Rights Apply?

**Outside business premises:**

**Distance selling:**
- Online shops

üí° **KEY POINT:** Buyer can withdraw **without giving reasons** and **without paying cancellation fee**.

---

### Withdrawal Periods

| Seller's Action | Buyer's Withdrawal Period |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| Seller INFORMS about right | **14 calendar days** after contract or delivery |
| Seller does NOT inform | **1 year + 14 days** |

**Example:** Jacket ordered online, delivered August 7th ‚Üí Can withdraw until August 21st.

---

### After Withdrawal

- Buyer pays return costs (if seller informed about this)
- If product was used/consumed, seller may demand compensation
- Seller must refund money received
